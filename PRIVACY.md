# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 10, 2026*

## Overview

ImageResizer is a macOS application for batch image resizing. Your privacy is important to us, and we've built the app with that in mind.

## Data Collection

ImageResizer does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app runs entirely on your Mac and does not communicate with any external servers.

## Image Processing

All image processing happens locally on your device. Your images are never uploaded anywhere. Resized images are saved to the same folder as the originals — nowhere else.

## EXIF Metadata

ImageResizer includes an option to strip EXIF metadata (such as GPS location, camera information, and timestamps) from your images. When enabled, metadata is removed during the resize process and is not stored or transmitted.

## Analytics & Tracking

ImageResizer does not include any analytics, tracking, crash reporting, or telemetry of any kind.

## Third-Party Services

ImageResizer does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.

## Settings Storage

Your preferences (such as resize mode, output format, and filename template) are saved locally on your Mac using macOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update this page with the new date.
